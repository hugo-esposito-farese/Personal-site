## What it does

You paste a conversation you had with an AI assistant — about a bug, a concept, or a system design question — and the app generates coding exercises that test whether you actually understood what was discussed.

It closes the loop between passive AI-assisted learning and active recall.

## How it works

1. Paste any AI conversation (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, etc.)
2. The system extracts the core concepts and topics covered
3. It generates targeted coding exercises matching your level and the content
4. You practice — without depending on the AI to hand you answers

## Current state

The app runs in mock mode — no paid AI APIs are required. It is fully functional as a demo product. The goal is to validate the concept before wiring up production models.
