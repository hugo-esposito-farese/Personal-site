## Why I built it

I was tired of having no public trail of what I was working on. My work existed in GitHub repos with no context, private notes, and conversations that disappeared. This site is the fix.

The reference I kept coming back to was Marc Lou's indiepa.ge — someone who documents the journey, not just the highlights.

## Design decisions

- Dark, minimal — inspired by Vercel, Linear, Apple
- Astro for speed and simplicity: no JS unless needed
- Tailwind v4 for styling
- Content lives in Markdown files — no CMS, no database, just files I can edit with any text editor

## What I learned

Building a personal site is an exercise in restraint. Every instinct says to add more. The right call is almost always to remove.

The hardest part was deciding what the site was *for*. Once I decided it was a build log and not a portfolio, every design decision got easier.
