First week at 42 Paris is done.

I expected something like school with a weird vibe. Instead it's more like being dropped into a problem with no instructions. The only way to understand what you're building is to build it.

The peer-to-peer model is strange at first. There are no teachers. You ask other students, read documentation, and figure it out. The frustrating part is also the point — the confusion is the curriculum.

What I noticed: everyone who succeeds here has a bias towards just *trying* things. The people who wait to fully understand before starting never finish.

I want to build products while I'm here. Not just code. Actual things people use. That's what this site is for — to keep myself accountable and document what happens.

Starting with the C pool. More updates soon.
